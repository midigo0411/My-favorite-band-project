Bien-Aime Baraza

bien-Anime (which means beloved in French) is the band’s songwriter, vocalist as well as a guitarist. He was born on December 28, 1987, making him 33 years old as of 2020. Bien is from the Abaluhyia tribe that hails from western Kenya. Bien was a student at the United States International University he studied Journalism and Media Studies. He graduated in 2011. Baraza has been an active member of the group. He put in a great effort in the creation of two albums by Sauti Sol which were: Mwanzo in 2008 and Sol Filosofia in 2011. He is also known to have written songs for artists like Wendy Kimani, Elani, and Amos & Josh. Many people were shocked to discover that Bien is the step-son of the defamed Deputy Chief Justice Nancy Baraza. The former judiciary deputy revealed this during an interview, a matter that she had managed to keep secret for a very long time.
